Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Open the Zivora app and tap "Sign Up". You can register with your email or use Google Sign-In for quick access.

Is my health data secure?

Yes, your data is encrypted and stored securely. We follow HIPAA compliance standards and never share your personal health information with third parties.

How do I log a migraine episode?

Tap "Log Symptoms" on the dashboard, select your pain level, triggers, and any medications taken. The app will save this to your health history.

Can I export my data for my doctor?

Yes! Go to Settings > Export Health Data to generate PDF or CSV reports that you can share with your healthcare provider.

How does the AI risk assessment work?

Our AI analyzes your logged symptoms, triggers, and patterns to provide a personalized risk score and insights about your migraine patterns.

Troubleshooting

App Not Working Properly?

  1. Force close the app and restart it
  2. Check your internet connection
  3. Update to the latest app version
  4. Restart your device
  5. Clear app cache (iOS: Offload and reinstall)
  6. Contact support if issues persist

Login Problems?

  1. Double-check your email and password
  2. Use "Forgot Password" to reset if needed
  3. Try signing in with Google if you registered that way
  4. Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  5. Contact support if you continue having issues
